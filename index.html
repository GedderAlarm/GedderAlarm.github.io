About Gedder

The Gedder Alarm clock is the next generation of wake-up technology. It is a smart and self-adjusting alarm that wakes you up just in time so you can get your morning routine covered and get to that important meeting in time despite traffic and public transportation delays.

It is by no means a regular alarm clock. In addition to your desired wake-up time, you tell Gedder where you will be departing from, where you are heading to, and when you need to be there, and Gedder will take care of the rest. Should any unfortunate events put obstacles in your route to world domination, Gedder Alarm will adjust your wake-up time to compensate for the delays so you can still make a quick stop at your favorite coffee shop.

In short, let Gedder help you GET THERE, in time!
